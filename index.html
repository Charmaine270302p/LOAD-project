ABOUT US

THE STORY OF WHO WE ARE

Found in 2008 and focused on providing cross-border transport across Zimbabwe,
Zambia, and South Africa.We are a BEE Level 6 certified firm, able to offer our clients
various options with regards to collections and delivery destinations.We have a sister
company in Zimbabwe operating three triaxles.

We have built up a wide customer base with our well-trained, well-presented and well-
mannered drivers.We operate a fully equipped workshop with highly trained staff in
Zimbabwe en-route for both companies with a 24 hours call out facility.

We are a small business which manages to do our best to provide a person and
professional service.
